# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want `t20-rpg-toolkit` to be a respectful, constructive, and creative environment for everyone participating in the project.

As contributors, maintainers, and collaborators, we pledge to make participation in this community harassment-free and professionally respectful for all people, regardless of background, identity, level of experience, or personal style.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- showing respect in technical disagreement
- giving direct but constructive feedback
- assuming good faith while still holding a high bar
- documenting decisions clearly
- helping keep the product direction coherent
- being careful with other people's time and work

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- insults, personal attacks, or dismissive behavior
- bad-faith review behavior
- hostile gatekeeping
- publishing private information without permission
- repeatedly derailing collaboration with noise or aggression

## Project expectations

Because this repository is product-driven and architecture-sensitive, contributors are also expected to:

- respect the documented workflow
- avoid direct work on `master`
- keep discussions tied to product and implementation reality
- avoid unnecessary chaos in scope, branch flow, or review process

Strong technical opinions are welcome.
Disrespect is not.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within:

- the GitHub repository
- pull requests and issues
- code reviews
- discussions related to the project
- other public or private project collaboration spaces

## Enforcement responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing acceptable standards of behavior.

They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, pull requests, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers through the repository communication channels.

All complaints will be reviewed and handled with seriousness and discretion appropriate to the situation.
